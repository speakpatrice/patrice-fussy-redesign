How Fussy Works

Fussy connects clients with hairstylists who specialize in Black women's hair textures styles. Our website features a growing community of stylists and a diverse photo gallery of hairstyles, including box braids, weaves and locs.

We are currently only listing stylists who are based in or travel to Brooklyn, New York.

We plan to expand Fussy to include stylists from across the nation ASAP! If you'd like Fussy to include your town or city, let us know!
